## Overview
AI RSB (Rwanda Smart Board) is an AI-powered digital platform designed to enhance tourism experiences and empower local businesses in Rwanda. The system leverages data-driven recommendations to help tourists discover authentic local experiences while enabling startups and small businesses to gain visibility and grow sustainably.

## Problem Statement
Tourists visiting Rwanda often struggle to fully experience what the country truly offers beyond popular destinations. At the same time, many local businesses and startups lack visibility, marketing reach, and access to influencers who could help accelerate their growth.

## Solution
AI RSB addresses these challenges by combining smart digital boards, AI-based recommendations, and cloud infrastructure to:
- Guide tourists to the best places based on their preferences
- Promote affordable and high-quality local services
- Empower startups and small businesses with visibility
- Connect new businesses with influencers for promotion

## Key Features
### Tourism Assistance
- Recommendations for best local places to visit
- Discovery of affordable hotels, restaurants, and hospitals with good service
- Access to cultural, food, and local experience data
- Personalized options based on tourist needs and preferences

### Local Business Empowerment
- Digital onboarding for local and startup businesses
- Smart board advertising for small and growing enterprises
- AI-driven recommendations to match businesses with relevant influencers
- Increased visibility for businesses that offer quality services at affordable prices

### Data & AI Intelligence
- Aggregation of tourism and local service data across Rwanda
- Ranking and recommendation based on service quality, affordability, and user demand
- Continuous learning from user interactions and feedback

## Technology Vision
- Cloud-hosted backend for scalability and reliability
- AI/ML models for recommendation and matching
- Web-based dashboards for businesses and administrators
- Smart board interfaces for public display and interaction

## Impact in Rwanda
- Enhances the tourist experience beyond common destinations
- Supports economic growth of local businesses
- Encourages digital transformation in tourism and commerce
- Promotes inclusive access to opportunities for startups

## Future Roadmap
- AI recommendation model implementation
- Mobile and web application development
- Integration with payment and booking platforms
- Partnerships with tourism boards, local businesses, and influencers
